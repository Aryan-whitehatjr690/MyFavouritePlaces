Prasads Mall In Hyderabad

Prasads mall at Necklace Road is proof that the first impression is the best impression.
Housing Hyderabad’s first multiplex and providing the city’s first taste of a metropolitan mall, it took time for people to look at it as an entertainment destination beyond movies.
Now, Hyderabadis are spoilt for choice amid a dozen other malls but nothing beats the feeling of being at good-old Prasads.
